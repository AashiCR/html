Meet the Team

Hugh Wade-Jones

Hugh founded Ennees in August 2007 with Isley Robinson and has grown it to be one of the world's leading advisers for high-value international debt. He is a widely regarded industry leader and was the winner of Best Broker for Large Loans for three consecutive years at the British Mortgage Awards.

He also is the founder of Tenn Capital, which is backed by the US Hedge Fund Elliott Management.

Outside of work, he can often be found running and cycling in the hills around Monaco and is an avid Tottenham Hotspur fan.
